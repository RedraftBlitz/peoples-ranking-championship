How the scores work

Season RB Value: Weeks 1–17 total minus the season total of the selected replacement-ranked player at that position.

Weekly RB Value: Each weekly score minus that week's replacement score at the position, summed across Weeks 1–17.

Volatility: Standard deviation of weekly scores, shown for analysis but not included automatically.
